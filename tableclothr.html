What is tableclothr?

tableclothr is a package which allows you to easily transform your data into static or dynamic tables. The original version was drafted by Josiah Ringelberg, and I have since grown it into a full fletched package.

Core Functionality

tableclothr integrates two of the most popular table creation packages together: gt and DT.

  1. gt follows in the footsteps of ggplot2, aiming to be a powerful, yet straightforward way of creating beatiful tables in R
  2. DT is an R interface for the JavaScript library, DataTables. Itâ€™s power comes from the ability to create html tables that allow you to interactively filter, sort, and search your data

tableclothr has one primary function: tablecloth().
